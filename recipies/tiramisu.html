Tiramisu Cake

Description

For when you're craving a smooth and creamy cake that gives you the bite of coffee, the smooth decadance of chocolate, and the sweeth tooth itch, look no futher than the tiramisu cake. Orignally made in Italy, the tiramisu has grown to become an international favorite after dinner, enjoyed with a small cup of coffee or with your favorite person.

Ingredients

Genoise Cake

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated white sugar (200g)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, sifted (120g)
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (7g)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee, espresso preferred - for brushing (120ml)

Mascarpone Frosting

  • 4 1/2 cups full-fat mascarpone, cold (2.2lbs / 1000g)
  • 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar, sifted (300g)
  • 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee, espresso preferred, chilled (120ml)
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream, cool (360ml)
  • unsweetened coca for dusting on top

Decoration for your preference

  • Whipped cream
  • Coca powder
