Cajun Shrimp Pasta

Description

When you're looking for a kick of heat or to enjoy a bold variet of flavors, cajun shrimp pasta is a favorite of many people. Popular in places like New Orleans, it really brings the fun and fun flavors that those communities bring to the world.

Ingredients

  1. 8oz penne pasta
  2. 1lb large peeled and deveined shrimp
  3. 1 Tbsp olive oil
  4. 2 Tbsp cajun seasoning, divided
  5. 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  6. 2 minced garlic cloves
  7. 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  8. 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  9. 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  10. 2 Tbsp parsley to garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the penne pasta in a pot of salted water until al dente, according to package instructions. Drain the water and cover the pasta to keep warm
  2. Coat the shrimp with 1 Tbsp of the cajun seasoning. In a deep skillet, heat 1 Tbsp oil over medium heat. Once hot, add the seasoned shrimp in a single layer and saute for about 1-2 minutes per side, or until the shrimp is no longer translucent, remove from skillet and cover to keep warm.
  3. In the same skillet, melt 2 Tbsp butter with the minced garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the diced tomatoes and saute for about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the heavy whipping cream, remaining 1 Tbsp cajun seasoning and grated parmesean cheese. Bring to a simmer then reduce to medium/low heat and continue 2-3 minutes or until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add the shrimp and pasta back into the skillet and stir until noodles are coated in sauce and everything is heated through. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired and serve.
  6. Enjoy!
